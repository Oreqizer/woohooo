WHO ARE WE?

We are a team of four women, who have found each other in search for an extraordinary life. We live life to the fullest, looking for adventure and thrill with the people we love the most. We believe in spontaneity and authenticity. We long to be real. To live in the moment, to make our dreams our reality. Each of us so very unique and talented, with the leadership of our driver “Woohooo” want to show you a different way of life. Be an example not only to young people, but anyone who has not given up on life, anyone not ready to accept the system, the Rebels! You will rarely meet us sitting at a bar or

at a coffee shop, we will rather meet you in the middle of the forest on quads, on the lake sailing ships, rafting, driving on circuits, go karting or simply on a picnic in the middle of a field. Always travelling to meet another adventure. Fighting the new age of virtual reality, wasting precious time with phones or tablets in your hands. Against women who sit around hating on each other, against women who can’t even cook, cannot take care for themselves. We want to wake you up, show you what life is made of, and mainly uncover the secret power you have no clue you have, that is hidden in every single one of you.
